Who Am I?

I'm Kristen the data ninja. Well not yet but I'm in intense training ala Uma Thurman in Kill Bill. Except my Pai Mei is the Data Science Immersive at General Assembly and it is thoroughly kicking my butt! In the best possible way of course.

I came to data science via investment banking, small business and an adtech startup. For a more formal bio, check out my LinkedIn.

Why data science?

For me translating messy data into real-life solutions is the key. Data is the new oil - the ultimate resource that will fuel the world. So why not learn to put it into good use?

I want to tell better stories, come up with better algorithms and use data science to improve my small corner of the world.
