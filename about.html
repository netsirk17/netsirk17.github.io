Who Am I?

I am currently in the Data Science Immersive at General Assembly. I am part of an intellectually curious yet slightly sadistic cohort all plodding through the depths of big data (and banging our heads on the tables) together.

Why data science?

For me translating messy data into real-life solutions is the key. Data is the new oil - the ultimate resource that will fuel the world. So why not learn to put it into good use?

What's the point?

I want to tell better stories, come up with better algorithms and use data science to improve my small corner of the world.
